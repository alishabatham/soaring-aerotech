## Sabse Badi Mistake Jo Avoid Karni Hai

Website ko **"Course Selling Website"** mat banana.

Website ko **"Drone Ecosystem Website"** banana.

Visitor ko ye feel aana chahiye:

> "Ye sirf training institute nahi hai. Ye drone technology, innovation, projects aur careers ka complete ecosystem hai."

Ye positioning hi Soaring Aerotech ki brand value ko 2–3 levels upar le jayegi aur MP ke zyadatar drone institutes se alag khada karegi.
